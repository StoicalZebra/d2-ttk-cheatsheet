# Destiny 2 Weapon Tuning Posts Archive

A compilation of TWAB (This Week At Bungie) and TWID (This Week In Destiny) posts containing weapon tuning, sandbox changes, and balance updates.

> **Note:** Bungie renamed "This Week At Bungie" (TWAB) to "This Week In Destiny" (TWID) in mid-2023.

---

## 2025

### Renegades (December 2025)

| Date | Title | Notes |
|------|-------|-------|
| 2025-11-11 | [Dev Insights - Renegades Weapon Tuning Preview](https://www.bungie.net/7/en/News/Article/dev_insight_renegades_weapons) | **Major Dev Insights** - First of three articles on Renegades sandbox. Covers ammo system, archetype balance, Exotic weapons, perks. |
| 2025-11-26 | [This Week in Destiny - 11/26/2025](https://www.bungie.net/7/en/News/Article/twid_11_26_2025) | Pre-launch TWID |

**Key Renegades Changes:**
- Mint Retrograde: Reserve ammo reduced, ammo per brick reduced (was in Kinetic slot 45% of time)
- Ace of Spades: Memento Mori PvE bonus +18% → +30%, Firefly damage 65 → 100
- Hawkmoon: Added PvE damage bonus per Paracausal Charge stack
- HUD buff priority iteration (important buffs no longer hidden)

### Ash & Iron (September 2025)

| Date | Title | Notes |
|------|-------|-------|

- New Exotics: New Malpais (Pulse Rifle), Wolfsbane (Axe)

| Date | Title | Notes |

## 2024

### Episode Revenant (October 2024)

| Date | Title | Notes |
|------|-------|-------|
| 2024-09-26 | [This Week in Destiny - 09/26/2024](https://www.bungie.net/7/en/News/article/twid_09_26_2024) | Episode Revenant preview - Crucible update plans, sandbox changes |
| 2024-09-19 | [This Week in Destiny - 09/19/2024](https://destiny.bungie.org/bwu/527) | Pre-Revenant sandbox preview |
| 2024-11-14 | [This Week in Destiny - 11/14/2024](https://www.bungie.net/7/en/News/Article/twid-11-14-24) | Mid-season tuning |

**Key Revenant Changes:**
- Primary Weapons: +5-7% critical hit damage, reduced body shot damage
- Sniper Rifles PvE: Minors +60%, Majors +75%, Minibosses +35%, Champions +25%
- Rocket-Assisted Sidearms: Reserve ammo -25%
- Heavy Burst Pulse Rifles: RPM increased, damage -18% (10% DPS drop overall)
- Shotguns PvE: 40% minimum damage at long range (up from 0%)
- High Impact Machine Guns: +4% critical damage

### The Final Shape (June 2024)

| Date | Title | Notes |
|------|-------|-------|
| 2024-06-04 | [The Final Shape Launch](https://www.bungie.net/7/en/news/article/d2_intothelight_tfs_recap) | Major expansion sandbox overhaul |
| 2024-05-30 | [This Week in Destiny - 05/30/2024](https://www.bungie.net/7/en/News/article/twid-05-30-24) | Pre-TFS sandbox preview |

**Key TFS Changes:**
- Pulse Rifles: Two consecutive buffs, strong PvE DPS option
- Chain Reaction: -15% detonation radius, -20% damage on Special weapons, +30% on Heavy
- Chill Clip: Partial nerf rollback (only Riptide requires 3 shots to freeze)

| Date | Title | Notes |

**Key Into the Light Changes:**
- Rapid Fire Pulse Rifles: +3.5% body damage, +1% crit damage
- Lightweight Pulse Rifles: +6% body damage
